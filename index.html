About Me

My name is Robert Gjorgjioski and I’m a passionate and hardworking front-end developer based in Skopje, Macedonia. Since a very young age I have discovered my love for everything about IT, computers have always been a part of my life. My journey started with testing, researching and ‘playing’ with UNIX/LINUX consoles back in 2010. From there on I also experimented with ethical hacking and creating (poor) websites.

Education:

Currently I am studying Civil Engineering at the SS. Cyril and Methodius University. My love for computers and everything related to IT has not disappeared, it only grew larger… For this reason, I started a course at GSIX Academy for front-end development. As soon as the pandemic hit and everyone started working remote, I began my journey as a freelancer. My current client base is mainly from Sweden.

Professional skills:

  • University-based work with multiple programming languages (C, C++),
  • CSS/SASS (animations, styling) also CSS Frameworks (Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS etc.),
  • Javascript/jQuery,
  • The Progressive JavaScript Framework Vue.js
  • Basic knowledge in Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD,
  • Responsive Web Design,
  • WordPress (mainly on theme development using Elementor and other building tools),
  • Knowledge of Linux systems and experience working with different Linux distributions (Arch Linux, Debian, Fedora, etc).

Personal skills:

  • Creative spirit,
  • Reliable and professional,
  • Organized,
  • Time management,
  • Team player,
  • Fast learner,
  • Motivated.

Goals:

My goal and responsibility as a web developer is implementing modern visual elements that users find attractive, but most importantly easy to use. I get satisfaction from filling the gap between graphical design and technical implementation. Taking responsibility and being actively involved on both sides is important to me. It is not only defining how the application looks, but also how it works.

Other hobbies not related to programming:

  • Cooking,
  • Swimming,
  • Gaming ( only multiplayer games for ex. CS:GO, League of Legends, Dota 2 etc.. ),
  • Driving alone with good loud music,
  • Good coffe and people around me to drink it.
